Our Story

A passion for exceptional cuisine

At La Maison, we believe that dining is more than a meal — it is a journey of the senses. Founded in 2018 by Chef Antoine Dubois, our restaurant was born from a deep love for French culinary tradition and a desire to reimagine it for the modern world.

Every dish on our menu is crafted from the finest seasonal ingredients, sourced directly from local farmers and artisans who share our commitment to quality and sustainability.

Antoine Dubois
Executive Chef & Founder
